About the Chubby Golem

The author behind the scenes is a generalist who has dabbled in many different fields. He has a Masters degree in mathematics and a PhD in Biochemistry. His studies focused mostly on in-vivo and in-vitro evolution of nucleic acids and software to make sense of the wet lab messes he created. Outside of academia has worked on a farm, at NASA, in a hospital, and at a bank. His hobbies include making dust with a CNC machine, programming, and outdoor activities. Of all the things he has done, he enjoys watching his daughter discover the world around her the most.
